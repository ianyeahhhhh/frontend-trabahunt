Need Help?

1. How to request for resources/employees?

Step 1: Go to Request dropdown.
Step 2: Click 'Create request'.
Step 3: Provide the details of the job.
Step 4: Click 'send request' button and wait for the response of the admin and HR.

2. Where can I see the status of my request?

Step 1: Go to Request dropdown.
Step 2: Click 'Request history'.
Step 3: Click the eye icon from the 'actions' column of the specific request you want to view.
(Details about your request are displayed.)

3. Where can I view the list of employees from HR?

Step 1: Go to Employees.
Step 2: Click the eye icon from the 'actions' column of the specific employee you want to view.
(Details about the employee are displayed.)
