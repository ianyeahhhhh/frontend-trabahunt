Need Help?

1. How to post job advertisement?

Step 1: Go to Job Advertisement dropdown.
Step 2: Choose Create Job Ads.
Step 3: Fill out the needed information.
Step 4: Click Save.
Step 5: Wait for approval.

2. How to search for a specific candidate?

Step 1: Go to Search Applicants.
Step 2: Click the eye button at the action part to view details.
(You can offer them a job position right after).

3. Where can I view the details of my pending job advertisement?

Step 1: Go to Job Advertisement dropdown.
Step 2: Choose Pending Job Ads.
Step 3: Wait for approval.
(You can view your pending job ads by clicking the eye button).

4. Where can I view the list of job applicants?

Step 1: Go to Job Advertisement dropdown.
Step 2: Choose Job Ads List.
Step 3: Click the blue button to view applicants of the specific job ads.
(You can view their status as well).
